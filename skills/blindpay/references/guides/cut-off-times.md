# Cut-off Times

## ACH

BlindPay provides same-day ACH processing for users requiring expedited electronic fund transfers. To ensure same-day settlement, all transactions must be submitted before our designated cutoff windows.

### Timing

| Batch Cutoff | Bank Processing | Estimated Settlement |
|--------------|-----------------|----------------------|
| 9:00 AM ET | Same business day | Same business day |
| 12:00 PM ET | Same business day | Same business day |

For transactions exceeding $50,000, BlindPay will need to convert your stablecoins to USD first, which may extend the processing time by one additional business day.

### Limits

Nacha, the governing body for ACH transactions, has established a maximum limit of $1,000,000 per transaction for same-day ACH payments.

## Wire

BlindPay offers wire transfers as a premium payment solution for high-value and time-critical transactions.

### Timing

| Cutoff Time | Bank Processing | Estimated Settlement |
|-------------|-----------------|----------------------|
| 3:00 PM ET | Same business day | Same business day |

For transactions exceeding $50,000, processing time may be extended by one additional business day.

## International SWIFT

BlindPay provides international wire transfers through the SWIFT network for secure cross-border payments.

### Timing

| Cutoff Time | Bank Processing | Estimated Settlement |
|-------------|-----------------|----------------------|
| 10:30 AM ET | Same business day | 1-5 business days |

BlindPay will always convert your stablecoins to USD first, which may extend the processing time by one additional business day.
